**Smart Vyapari – CodeInventory** is a full-stack, modular Inventory Management System (IMS) designed to digitize and streamline stock operations for businesses. It replaces manual registers and scattered Excel sheets with a centralized, real-time platform.

---

## **Features**

- **Product Management:** Create, update, categorize products, and track stock per warehouse.
- **Receipts:** Manage incoming stock from suppliers and auto-update inventory.
- **Delivery Orders:** Handle outgoing stock for customers, including pick, pack, and validation.
- **Internal Transfers:** Move stock between warehouses or racks, fully logged in a ledger.
- **Stock Adjustments:** Reconcile physical stock counts with system records.
- **Dashboard & KPIs:** Real-time insights into total stock, low-stock items, pending deliveries, and internal transfers.
- **Alerts & Filters:** Low-stock notifications, SKU search, dynamic filters by warehouse, category, or status.
- **Multi-Warehouse Support:** Efficiently manage multiple locations.
- **Stock Ledger:** All inventory movements are tracked for accountability and audits.

---

## **Tech Stack**

- **Frontend:** React.js + TailwindCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Authentication:** JWT + OTP-based password reset
- **Hosting:** Vercel (frontend), Render/Heroku (backend)
